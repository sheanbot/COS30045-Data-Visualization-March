Quick Energy Saving Tips

  • Enable Eco-Mode: Most modern TVs automatically dim the backlight based on room lighting.
  • Disable Standby: Use a smart power strip to cut power entirely when the TV isn't in use.
  • Brightness: Vivid mode can consume up to 30% more power than Standard or Movie modes.
